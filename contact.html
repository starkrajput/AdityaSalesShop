Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer product warranty?

Yes, all our products come with manufacturer's warranty. Laptops typically have 1-3 years warranty depending on the model, while camera accessories usually come with 1 year warranty.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and EMI options through various banks. For specific EMI plans, please contact our store directly.

Do you offer doorstep delivery?

Yes, we offer free doorstep delivery for orders within Varanasi city. For deliveries outside Varanasi, shipping charges may apply depending on the location and product weight.

Can I exchange my old laptop for a new one?

Yes, we do have exchange offers for eligible laptops. The exchange value depends on the condition, age, and model of your device. Please bring your device to our store for a free evaluation.
